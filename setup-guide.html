📺 Watch At Home – Setup Guide

Follow these steps to install the app and use your login details:

1️⃣ Install the App

Download Smarters Pro Player from:

  • 🔹 Google Play Store (Android)
  • 🔹 App Store (iPhone/iPad)

Or directly from the official website:

👉 iptvsmarters.com/#downloads

2️⃣ Open the App

Inside the app:

  1. Click on "Login with Xtream Codes API"
  2. Enter your login info (you received it in a separate message)
  3. Click "Add User" and enjoy 🎉

📺 For Smart TVs (Android TV, LG, Samsung)

To use the service on your Smart TV, follow these steps:

  1. Open your TV’s App Store (Google Play, LG Content Store, or Samsung Store).
  2. Search for IPTV Smarters Pro.
  3. ✅ The correct app has a dark blue icon with the text Smarters PRO on it.
  4. Install it and open the app.

⚠️ Important: You may see other apps with similar names like:

  • IPTV Smarters Lite
  • Smarters Player Live
  • Max Smarters TV

❌ Do NOT install these. They are not official and may not work properly.

If you're not sure which one is correct, just send us a photo and we’ll help you choose the right one ✅
